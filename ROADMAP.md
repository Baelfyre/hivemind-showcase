# Roadmap

This roadmap is a public, high-level summary. It is not a commitment and may
change as the product evolves.

## Current Focus

- Public product documentation.
- Workspace collaboration quality.
- Clear separation between public, workspace, admin, and service surfaces.
- Reliability and reviewability across product flows.

## Near-Term Themes

- Improve public website clarity.
- Refine guided collaboration workflows.
- Expand workspace documentation and examples.
- Improve administrative visibility and support workflows.
- Strengthen protected service reliability.

## Future Themes

- More collaboration templates.
- Team-oriented workflows.
- Better public examples and walkthroughs.
- Clearer product education for different user roles.

## Notes

The showcase roadmap intentionally avoids private operational plans,
implementation sequencing, and protected system details.
