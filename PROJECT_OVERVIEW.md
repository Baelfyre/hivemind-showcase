# Project Overview

HiveMind is a collaboration product for people who use AI to plan, research,
write, code, and analyze complex work.

The core idea is simple: give users a structured workspace where they can start
from a clear intent, choose a collaboration pattern, and keep the session
organized as the work develops.

## Product Goals

- Make AI collaboration easier to start.
- Support structured thinking instead of one-off answers.
- Keep user judgment central.
- Provide workspace continuity across longer sessions.
- Separate public, user, administrative, and service responsibilities.

## Product Surfaces

HiveMind is organized into distinct surfaces:

- A public website for product information, support, and legal content.
- A workspace for user collaboration sessions.
- An administrative area for operations and oversight.
- A protected service layer for sensitive actions.
- A public showcase for high-level documentation.

## Showcase Scope

This showcase is intentionally limited. It presents public product context and
does not include production software, service internals, protected workflows, or
operational setup.
